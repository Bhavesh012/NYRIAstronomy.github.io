--- layout: member_profile title: "Members" permalink: /members/bos/ name: "Steven Bos" affiliation: "Leiden University" country: "Netherlands" position: "PhD Student" title: "The vector-APP coronagraph as integral part of a spectropolarimetric high-contrast imaging system" summary: "Steven Bos is a PhD student working under the supervision of Frans Snik. He is developing the vector Apodizing Phase Plate (vAPP) coronagraph into an integral part of a high-contrast imaging system. This means that the vAPP will be designed to be integrated with focal-plane wavefront sensing (fpWFS), polarimetry and spectroscopy by leveraging the unique properties of liquid-crystal technology. This will enable unambiguous characterization of exoplanet atmospheres and surfaces.
