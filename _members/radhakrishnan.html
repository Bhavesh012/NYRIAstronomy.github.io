Current Research Summary:

I research in the field of adaptive optics. Wavefront sensing and reconstruction, AO calibration, predictive control using AO telemetry are a few topics I enjoy doing research, in addition to the actual hands-on work in optics.
Projects include: LINC-NIRVANA, INGOT, MAVIS, SHARK-NIR, MAORY.
